Our Breakthrough Technology

FT-YOLO OTA 5.0 system with Spatial-Semantic Separation Theory (SSDT) enables unprecedented performance in UAV thermal imaging

Spatial-Semantic Separation Theory

Our proprietary SSDT framework resolves the fundamental conflict between feature preservation and computational efficiency in thermal imaging. By decoupling spatial and semantic information pathways, we maintain geometric accuracy while optimizing processing speed.

Hierarchical Adaptive Downsampling

Our HASD technology achieves Pareto optimality in accuracy-speed trade-offs by maintaining information entropy retention rates above 95%, dramatically improving small target detection while reducing computational requirements.

Online Evolutionary Learning

Our OTA learning engine enables incremental model updates on embedded UAV CPUs in just 0.47 seconds - an 84.4% improvement over conventional techniques - with minimal bandwidth requirements (<100 kbps).

CPU-Neural Network Co-Design

Our Hardware-Algorithm Co-optimization Architecture (HACA) delivers real-time performance of 75.3 FPS at less than 15W power consumption on Intel NUC platforms, enabling extended mission durations.
